## 0.1.2

* Initial release.
* `PullToReveal` widget with universal child support (scrollable and non-scrollable).
* Three reveal modes: `slide`, `fade`, `scale`.
* Rubber-band resistance physics with logarithmic curve.
* `PullToRevealController` for programmatic reveal and dismiss.
* `onReveal`, `onCancel`, `onPull` callbacks.
* Haptic feedback on threshold trigger.
* Full unit and widget test suite.
